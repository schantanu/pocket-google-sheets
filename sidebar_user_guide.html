User Guide

Welcome to the Pocket App for Google Sheets. This guide will help you manage your Pocket articles efficiently.

Initial Setup

To begin using the application:

  1. Access the "Pocket App Guide" from the "Pocket App" menu
  2. Follow the step-by-step instructions to connect your Pocket account
  3. Once connected, you'll have access to all features
Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues with the sheets:

  1. Click "Setup/Reset Sheets" in the menu
  2. Wait for the reset to complete
  3. Your sheets will be restored to their default state

This will only reset the sheet structure, not your Pocket connection

Downloading Articles

To import your Pocket articles:

  1. Click "Get Articles" in the menu
  2. Wait for the import to complete
  3. Your articles will appear in the "Articles" sheet

All article metadata will be automatically populated

Adding New Articles

To save new articles to Pocket:

  1. Go to the "Add Articles" sheet
  2. Enter the article URL in Column A
  3. Optionally add tags in Column B (separate multiple tags with commas)
  4. Click "Add Articles" in the menu to upload the articles to Pocket

You can add multiple articles at once

Removing Articles

To delete articles from your Pocket account:

  1. Select articles to remove in the "Articles" sheet
  2. Delete the entire row(s)
  3. Click "Update Articles" in the menu

Currently, only article deletion is supported through the Update function
